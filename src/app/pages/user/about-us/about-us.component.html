Product Description

The Auto Broker app is a powerful and user-friendly platform designed to simplify car buying and selling for individuals and dealerships. It offers a seamless experience for posting, browsing, and managing car advertisements.

With advanced search filters, real-time chat features, and smart recommendation systems, our app connects buyers with the perfect vehicle and helps sellers find the right customers with ease. Whether you're looking for a sedan, SUV, or a used vehicle, Auto Broker has you covered.

Key Features and Benefits:

  • Real-time Chat Integration: Connect buyers and sellers instantly through a secure chat feature built into the app.
  • Advanced Filters: Search by brand, model, fuel type, year, price, and more to find exactly what you need.
  • Image Gallery Carousel: View multiple car images in an intuitive, swipe-enabled gallery.
  • Admin Dashboard: Manage users, listings, and reported ads with role-based access control.
  • Location-Based Search: Discover vehicles posted near you using location filters with support for all Sri Lankan cities.

The Auto Broker app also supports multilingual interfaces, user account registration, secure payment integration, and personalized dashboard views for buyers and sellers.

Whether you're a car dealer or an individual seller, our app provides all the tools you need to reach a larger audience and manage your ads effectively. Get started with Auto Broker today and drive your car deals forward!
